# Preventing Developer Burnout

Burnout is real and devastating. Vibecoding prioritizes sustainable development to prevent it.

## Recognizing Burnout

### Signs to Watch For
- Chronic fatigue
- Cynicism about work
- Reduced performance
- Detachment from team
- Physical symptoms (headaches, insomnia)

### Team-Level Indicators
- Increased conflict
- Higher turnover
- Missed deadlines
- Quality decline
- Loss of creativity

## Prevention Strategies

### 1. Sustainable Pace
- Realistic deadlines
- Adequate planning
- Buffer time for the unexpected
- Respect for personal time

### 2. Psychological Safety
- Open communication
- No blame culture
- Safe to admit mistakes
- Supportive feedback

### 3. Autonomy and Mastery
- Meaningful work
- Growth opportunities
- Decision-making authority
- Learning resources

### 4. Connection and Community
- Team bonding activities
- Mentorship programs
- Peer support
- Social events

### 5. Physical and Mental Health
- Regular breaks
- Encouraged time off
- Mental health resources
- Ergonomic workspaces

## Leadership Responsibilities

Leaders must:
- Model healthy behaviors
- Check in regularly
- Remove obstacles
- Provide resources
- Celebrate achievements

## Emergency Response

When burnout occurs:
- Immediate support
- Reduced workload
- Professional help if needed
- Recovery plan
- Reintegration support

## The Vibe Check

Burnout prevention isn't just nice—it's essential. Healthy teams build better software and sustain it longer.
