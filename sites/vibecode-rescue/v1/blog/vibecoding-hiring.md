# Hiring with Vibecoding

Hiring is your opportunity to build a team that embodies vibecoding principles from the start.

## Traditional Hiring Problems

- Focus on technical skills only
- Cultural mismatch
- Toxic behavior tolerated
- Short-term thinking
- Poor onboarding

## Vibecoding Hiring Principles

### 1. Values First
Hire for values, train for skills:
- Empathy
- Collaboration
- Growth mindset
- Positive attitude
- Respect for others

### 2. Cultural Fit
Assess cultural alignment:
- Team interaction style
- Communication approach
- Conflict resolution
- Learning orientation
- Work-life balance

### 3. Potential Over Experience
Look for growth potential:
- Learning ability
- Adaptability
- Curiosity
- Problem-solving
- Passion for craft

### 4. Inclusive Process
Ensure fair evaluation:
- Structured interviews
- Diverse interviewers
- Clear criteria
- Bias awareness
- Consistent process

## Interview Process

### Technical Assessment
- Practical problems
- Pair programming
- Code review simulation
- System design discussion
- Architecture reasoning

### Cultural Assessment
- Behavioral questions
- Team interaction
- Scenario-based questions
- Values exploration
- Reference checks

### Collaborative Assessment
- Group exercises
- Team lunch
- Office hours
- Open discussion
- Q&A with team

## Red Flags

### Technical Red Flags
- Can't explain their code
- Rigid about approaches
- Blames others
- No curiosity
- Resists feedback

### Cultural Red Flags
- Arrogant or dismissive
- Poor listener
- Negative about past teams
- Values competition over collaboration
- Lacks empathy

## Green Flags

### Technical Green Flags
- Explains reasoning clearly
- Admits what they don't know
- Asks good questions
- Learns from feedback
- Enjoys teaching

### Cultural Green Flags
- Positive and enthusiastic
- Good listener
- Respects different perspectives
- Values collaboration
- Shows empathy

## Onboarding Integration

Connect hiring to onboarding:
- Smooth transition
- Buddy assignment
- Clear expectations
- Early wins
- Cultural integration

## Measuring Hiring Success

Track outcomes:
- Performance quality
- Cultural fit
- Retention rates
- Team satisfaction
- Promotion rates

## The Vibe Check

Hiring is about building a team, not filling positions. Take the time to find people who will thrive in your vibecoding culture.
